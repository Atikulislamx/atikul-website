# Services Documentation

## Project

ATIKUL WEBSITE

Official Website of **Atikul Islam Rabbi**

---

# Purpose

This document defines the services offered through the website, their structure, content requirements, user journey, and design expectations.

The objective is to help visitors understand available services, establish trust, answer common questions, and encourage consultation requests.

---

# Service Philosophy

Every service page should focus on:

* Education before promotion
* Clear explanations
* Professional tone
* Honest expectations
* Practical guidance
* Building trust

Avoid exaggerated marketing claims or guarantees.

---

# Standard Service Page Structure

Every service page should include:

1. Hero Section
2. Service Overview
3. Common Problems
4. How I Can Help
5. Process
6. Benefits
7. Frequently Asked Questions
8. Related Blog Articles
9. Call to Action
10. Contact Section

Maintain a consistent layout across all services.

# Service Layout Components

Every service page should include:

## Hero

* Service title
* Short introduction
* CTA button

---

## Overview

Explain:

* What the service is
* Who it is for
* When it may be useful

---

## Common Challenges

Describe common situations visitors may face.

Examples:

* Lost access
* Security concerns
* Permission issues
* Verification questions

Avoid promising specific outcomes.

---

## Process

Provide a high-level explanation of the consultation process.

Example structure:

1. Initial discussion
2. Situation review
3. Information gathering
4. Guidance
5. Follow-up recommendations

---

## Benefits

Highlight benefits such as:

* Better understanding
* Improved account organization
* Stronger security practices
* Clearer documentation
* Reduced risk

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

Each service page should include FAQs.

Suggested topics:

* Who is this service for?
* What information should I prepare?
* How long does a consultation take?
* What platforms are supported?
* How do I get started?

---

## Related Articles

Display relevant blog posts based on the service topic.

Example:

Business Verification page

Related articles:

* Preparing for Business Verification
* Common Verification Mistakes
* Meta Business Updates

---

## Contact Section

Provide:

* Consultation button
* Contact form
* Email
* Social links

# Success Criteria

A successful service page should help visitors:

Understand the service.

Know whether it matches their needs.

Learn practical information.

Trust the professional expertise presented.

Feel confident about requesting a consultation.

Every service page should educate first, build credibility second, and invite contact naturally.
