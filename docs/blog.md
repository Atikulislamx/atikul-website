# Blog Documentation

## Project

ATIKUL WEBSITE

Official Website of **Atikul Islam Rabbi**

---

# Purpose

The blog is a core component of the website.

Its primary purpose is to educate readers, establish professional authority, improve search engine visibility, and provide useful resources related to social media security, Meta platforms, and digital presence.

The blog should always prioritize accuracy, clarity, and usefulness over promotional content.

---

# Goals

The blog should help to:

* Educate visitors
* Build trust
* Demonstrate expertise
* Improve SEO
* Support Google Knowledge Panel
* Attract organic traffic
* Encourage consultation requests
* Create long-term evergreen content

---

# Target Audience

The blog is written for:

* Business owners
* Entrepreneurs
* Content creators
* Facebook Page administrators
* Instagram creators
* Digital marketers
* Freelancers
* Agencies
* Students interested in social media management

---

# Writing Style

Every article should be:

* Professional
* Clear
* Friendly
* Educational
* Practical
* Well-structured
* Easy to understand

Avoid clickbait, sensationalism, and unsupported claims.

---

# Blog Categories

The website should organize articles into categories such as:

* Meta Updates
* Facebook
* Instagram
* Business Manager
* Social Media Security
* Digital Asset Recovery
* Google Knowledge Panel
* SEO
* Digital Marketing
* Artificial Intelligence
* Tutorials
* Guides
* Industry News

Each article should belong to one primary category and may include relevant tags.

---

# Article Structure

Every article should follow a consistent structure:

1. Featured Image
2. Title
3. Short Introduction
4. Table of Contents (optional for long articles)
5. Main Content
6. Key Takeaways
7. Frequently Asked Questions (when appropriate)
8. Related Articles
9. Call to Action

---

# Metadata

Each article should include:

* Title
* Slug
* Category
* Tags
* Author
* Publication Date
* Last Updated
* Estimated Reading Time
* Featured Image
* Meta Description

---

# Article Length

Recommended ranges:

* Short: 600–900 words
* Standard: 1,000–1,800 words
* In-depth Guides: 2,000–3,500 words

Length should match the complexity of the topic rather than an arbitrary target.

---

# Content Types

Publish a mix of:

* News updates
* How-to guides
* Tutorials
* Explanatory articles
* FAQs
* Best practices
* Opinion pieces (clearly identified as opinion)
* Case studies (with client privacy protected)

---

# Internal Linking

Every article should naturally link to:

* Related blog posts
* Relevant service pages
* About page
* Consultation page
* Contact page

Use descriptive anchor text.

---

# Images

Use images that:

* Support the topic
* Are high quality
* Are optimized for web
* Include descriptive alt text

Avoid unnecessary decorative images.

---

# Featured Articles

The homepage should display the most recent or most important blog posts.

Featured articles should represent a variety of categories over time.

---

# Search & Navigation

The blog should support:

* Search
* Category filters
* Tag pages (future)
* Pagination
* Related posts

Visitors should be able to discover additional relevant content easily.

---

# SEO Best Practices

Every article should include:

* Unique title
* Unique meta description
* Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
* Descriptive URL
* Internal links
* Optimized images
* Clear introduction
* Helpful conclusion

Write for people first, then optimize for search engines.

---

# Publishing Workflow

Recommended process:

1. Choose a topic.
2. Research reliable sources.
3. Create an outline.
4. Write the draft.
5. Review for accuracy.
6. Add images.
7. Optimize SEO.
8. Publish.
9. Share on social platforms.
10. Update the article if new information becomes available.

---

# Updating Content

Some topics, especially platform updates, change over time.

When significant changes occur:

* Review the article.
* Update outdated information.
* Adjust screenshots if necessary.
* Revise the "Last Updated" date.

---

# Calls to Action

Each article should end with a relevant next step, such as:

* Explore a related service
* Read another article
* Contact for a consultation
* View additional resources

CTAs should be helpful and contextual.

---

# Quality Standards

Before publishing, verify that the article:

* Is factually accurate
* Is clearly written
* Avoids unnecessary repetition
* Uses proper grammar and spelling
* Provides practical value
* Respects copyright

---

# Future Features

Possible enhancements:

* Newsletter
* Reading progress bar
* Comments (optional)
* Related articles
* Popular posts
* Estimated reading time
* Social sharing
* Author bio
* RSS feed

---

# Success Criteria

A successful blog should:

* Answer real user questions.
* Be easy to read.
* Demonstrate expertise.
* Encourage visitors to explore more content.
* Support long-term SEO growth.
* Strengthen the online authority of Atikul Islam Rabbi.

Every article should leave readers with practical knowledge and a clear understanding of the topic while reinforcing the website as a trusted educational resource.
