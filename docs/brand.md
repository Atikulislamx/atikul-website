# Brand Guidelines

## Project

ATIKUL WEBSITE

Official Website of **Atikul Islam Rabbi**

---

# Brand Overview

Atikul Islam Rabbi is a Social Media Security Specialist focused on helping businesses, creators, and organizations secure and recover their digital assets across major social media platforms.

The website represents a professional personal brand built on trust, expertise, education, and long-term relationships with clients.

This website is not intended to function as a traditional portfolio. Instead, it serves as an authority website that combines professional services, educational resources, case studies, and industry insights.

---

# Mission

Help businesses and creators protect, recover, and strengthen their social media presence through professional guidance, practical solutions, and educational content.

---

# Vision

To become a trusted global authority in social media security, Meta Business Manager management, digital asset recovery, and online brand protection.

# Brand Personality

The brand should consistently communicate:

Professional

Knowledgeable

Helpful

Approachable

Reliable

Honest

Confident

Modern

Educational

Solution-Oriented

Avoid sounding arrogant, overly promotional, or sensational.

---

# Brand Voice

Content should be:

Clear

Simple

Professional

Respectful

Educational

Practical

Friendly

Confident

Every article, page, and service description should prioritize clarity and usefulness over marketing language.

---

# Positioning Statement

Atikul Islam Rabbi provides trusted guidance and practical solutions for businesses and creators seeking to protect, recover, and strengthen their social media presence.

The website should position the brand as a knowledgeable professional who combines technical expertise with clear communication.

---

# Visual Identity

The overall appearance should be:

Minimal

Modern

Professional

Elegant

Fast

Clean

Accessible

Readable

Avoid unnecessary visual complexity.

---

# Color Palette

Primary Colors

Black

White

Dark Gray

Accent Colors

Blue

Purple

Backgrounds should remain clean and uncluttered.

Accent colors should be used consistently to highlight important actions and key information.

---

# Typography

Typography should emphasize readability.

Use:

Large headings

Comfortable paragraph spacing

Consistent font hierarchy

Readable body text

Avoid decorative fonts.

---

# Imagery

Images should reflect professionalism and authenticity.

Preferred image types:

Professional portraits

Technology-themed photography

Workspaces

Minimal illustrations

Abstract technology backgrounds

Avoid:

Stock photos with exaggerated expressions

Overly dramatic effects

Low-quality graphics

Visual clutter

---

# Iconography

Use clean, modern icons.

Maintain consistent style throughout the website.

Icons should support content rather than distract from it.

---

# Photography Style

Professional

Natural

Well-lit

High resolution

Minimal editing

Consistent color grading

---

# Logo Usage

The logo should remain simple and recognizable.

Provide adequate spacing around the logo.

Never distort, stretch, or apply unnecessary visual effects.

---

# Website Objectives

The website should help visitors:

Understand who Atikul Islam Rabbi is.

Learn about available services.

Read educational blog content.

Review professional experience.

Explore case studies.

Request consultations.

Contact easily.

Trust the brand.

---

# Content Strategy

The website should emphasize education before promotion.

Educational content should answer common questions and provide practical guidance.

Marketing content should remain factual, respectful, and focused on value.

# Accessibility

The visual identity should remain accessible for all users.

Maintain sufficient contrast.

Support keyboard navigation.

Provide descriptive alternative text for images.

Use clear labels and headings.

---

# Long-Term Vision

This website should evolve into a comprehensive knowledge hub covering social media security, platform updates, educational resources, and professional services.

The goal is to become a trusted destination where visitors can both learn and seek professional guidance while reinforcing the credibility and authority of Atikul Islam Rabbi.
